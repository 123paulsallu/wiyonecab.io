# WiYone Cab - Ride Sharing Application

## App Description
WiYone Cab is a cutting-edge ride-sharing application that revolutionizes transportation in Sierra Leone. Our app connects drivers and riders seamlessly, providing safe, affordable, and reliable ride-sharing services.

## Key Features

### For Riders
- **Easy Ride Booking**: Book rides in seconds with an intuitive interface
- **Real-time Tracking**: Track your driver's location in real-time
- **Affordable Rates**: Competitive pricing with transparent cost breakdown
- **Driver Ratings**: View driver ratings and reviews before booking
- **Safe & Secure**: All transactions are encrypted and verified
- **History Tracking**: Keep a record of all your past rides

### For Drivers
- **Flexible Work**: Set your own schedule and work on your terms
- **Real-time Earnings**: Track your daily earnings instantly
- **Rider Information**: Know your rider details before accepting rides
- **Multiple Payments**: Secure payment processing and withdrawals
- **Performance Dashboard**: Monitor your ratings and statistics

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Is WiYone Cab available in my city?
**A**: WiYone Cab is currently available in Kenema, Sierra Leone. We are expanding to more cities soon.

### Q: How do I become a driver?
**A**: Visit the driver signup section, complete the registration, and await verification approval.

### Q: Is my payment information safe?
**A**: Yes, all payments are processed securely through encrypted channels with industry-standard protocols.

### Q: Can I cancel a ride after booking?
**A**: Yes, you can cancel rides before a driver accepts. After acceptance, contact support for cancellation.

### Q: How are fares calculated?
**A**: Fares are based on distance and time. You'll see the estimated fare before confirming the ride.
